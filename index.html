Guatemala City, Guatemala

Guatemala is known as the country of endless spring. It has over 20 mayan languages. It is Central America's most populous country. It has over 30 volcanoes. The volcano named Tajumulco is the tallest in all of Central America. Out of all 30 volcanoes, only 3 are active: Fuego, Pacaya and Santiaguito. It is the vibrant heart of the ancient Maya civilization. Coffee is one of the country's main exports and sources of income.
